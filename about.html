About ForKeepSakes

At ForKeepSakes, this isn’t just about woodworking — it’s about building something deeper. What started as a journey with zero experience and a pile of scrap wood has become a growing mission: to create with purpose, share the process, and bring people together through craft.

What I'm Building

  • Build-Your-Own Kits – Easy-to-follow kits designed for bonding time, first-time builders, or just a peaceful afternoon of making something real.
  • Seasonal Projects – Keepsakes that grow with the year. Think Halloween boxes, Mother's Day gifts, winter-themed shelves — all designed to inspire memories.
  • Woodworking Plans – From simple beginner projects to more advanced builds as I grow in my craft, these plans will be downloadable and designed to help you learn while creating something meaningful.

The Heart Behind the Work

ForKeepSakes was born from more than just a love of wood — it came from a need to heal, to grow, and to share that growth. Every cut, every joint, every moment in the shop is part of a bigger message: progress over perfection. I’ve learned the value of mindfulness in the making, and how focusing on craft can help quiet the chaos.

10% of every sale goes toward supporting mental health awareness — because sometimes building a box is really about rebuilding yourself.

Always Forward

This site — and this journey — is a work in progress. Just like each of us. Whether you're here to build, to support, or just to see what's next, you're part of something that's always moving forward.

Let’s make something that matters. Let’s make it ForKeepSakes.
