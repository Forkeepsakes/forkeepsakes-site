Acknowledgement

ForKeepSakes has always been more than wood and tools — it’s a reflection of family, memory, and the spirit of moving forward. This space would not exist without the love, patience, and encouragement of those who have walked beside me.

Every project carries their fingerprints in quiet ways: the lessons they taught, the laughter we shared, the strength they gave when times were heavy. Their presence is felt in every cut of wood, every joint fit together, and every story that gets told through what we create.

This page stands as a simple thank you — to family, to friends, and to the community that continues to inspire me. ForKeepSakes is not just mine; it belongs to every hand and every heart that has helped shape it.

Always Forward — Always ForKeepSakes — Always Family.
